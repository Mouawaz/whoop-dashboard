Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Whoop Dashboard ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our service.

Information Collection

We collect information from your Whoop account through the Whoop API, but only with your explicit permission through the OAuth authentication process. This includes:

  • Recovery information
  • Cycle information
  • Sleep information
  • Workout information
  • Profile information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Display your Whoop data on your personal dashboard
  • Analyze trends in your data
  • Provide insights about your performance and recovery

Data Storage

Your data is stored in JSON files in a GitHub repository. Access to this data is limited to you and anyone you choose to share your repository with.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. Your data is used exclusively for display on your personal dashboard.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
